Tomato Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is one of the most classic spaghetti recipes, cherished by pasta lovers around the world.

It captures the essence of Italian cuisine with its robust flavors and straightforward preparation.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225g) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)
  1. Cook the spaghetti:

    2. Fill a large pot with water, add a pinch of salt, and bring it to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

  2. Sauté the garlic:

    3. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Be careful not to burn it.

  3. Add the tomatoes and herbs:

    4. Pour the crushed tomatoes into the skillet with the garlic. Stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine all the flavors.

  4. Simmer the sauce:

    5. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.

  5. Combine the spaghetti and sauce:

    6. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss everything together gently, ensuring that the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. Serve and garnish:

    7. Transfer the tomato spaghetti to serving plates. If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves for an extra pop of flavor. Offer grated Parmesan cheese on the side for those who want to add a cheesy touch.

  7. Enjoy:

    8. Serve this classic tomato spaghetti warm and enjoy every mouthful of its exquisite flavors. Pair it with a side salad or garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.
