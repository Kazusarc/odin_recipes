Maruchan

Description

just the best food ever made

Ingredients

  • Maruchan
  • water (potable if you can)
  1. put the water to boil
  2. put the Maruchan on a really deep plate (or use the package as plate)
  3. put the water on the Maruchan and wait 2 minutes
  4. Enjoy one of the best food ever made
